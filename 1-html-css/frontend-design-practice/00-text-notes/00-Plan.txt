## **üí° Suggested Practice Strategy**

1. **Pick one component or concept at a time**
2. **Build multiple variations** (colors, spacing, typography, motion)

5. **Combine components** after mastering basic + medium + advanced
6. **Use SVG & Canvas** for interactive or animated elements
